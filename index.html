Life Story

Growing up in Oakland, I wasn't exposed to the many arcane pleasures in life, it being a more conservative city than most. One would assume that living in such a suffocating environment would have stunted a festive spirit such as I, but not even the cold malice of Oakland's many shadow fiends could darken the enclosure in which my soul resides.

Living in those conditions only made me more resolute, each article I penned serving as another rung on the ladder on which I climbed steadfastedly out of that molasses pit of a city. Nothing could have prepared me more for the ceaseless trials of adulthood.
